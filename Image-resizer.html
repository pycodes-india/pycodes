Frequently Asked Questions & User Guide

How to compress an image to exactly 50KB or 20KB?

Simply upload your passport photo or signature image into our online dropzone. Choose the standard 20KB or 50KB presets or set a custom size limit. Click 'Compress Image' and our algorithm will dynamically fine-tune the scale and pixel ratios to lock under your specified file target perfectly.

Is this Image Resizer secure for uploading personal documents?

Yes, this utility is 100% secure. It functions entirely inside your web browser (client-side processing). No data or photographs are ever uploaded to an external database, cloud service, or backend server. Your absolute privacy is guaranteed.

Which output format should I use for government/exam forms?

JPEG (or JPG) is universally recommended for standard student application portals. If you require higher graphic efficiency for newer platforms, you can switch the output engine to WEBP or lossless PNG formats inside the step 3 advanced panel configuration.

Why is my image resolution automatically changing?

To drop complex digital weights into tiny sizes like 10KB or 20KB while avoiding complete pixel distortion, the compression algorithm scales down physical dimensions proportionally. This provides optimization balanced perfectly for modern online applications.
