Exact Age Calculator

Calculate your precise age in years, months, and days for exam eligibility forms and applications.

Check official notifications (like UPSC, SSC, state police) for their specific cut-off date.

How to Use the Age Calculator

Whether you are filling out a government job application, a college admission form, or checking eligibility for exams, calculating your exact age can be tricky. This tool from pycodes.in makes it simple:

  • Step 1: Select your exact Date of Birth from the first calendar.
  • Step 2: Select the "As On" date. If an exam requires you to be a certain age by "1st July," enter that date. Otherwise, click "Today."
  • Step 3: Click "Generate Age Slip" to get a detailed breakdown of your age in years, months, days, and total weeks lived.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why is my age calculated differently for different exams?

Government exams and academic boards often set a specific "cut-off date" (e.g., January 1st or July 1st of the exam year). Your eligibility depends on your age exactly on that date, not today's date.

Does this calculator handle leap years?

Yes, the pycodes.in calculator uses built-in calendar logic to accurately account for leap years and months with 28, 29, 30, or 31 days.
