Frequently Asked Questions

How does this work offline?

The entire algorithm runs locally in your browser using vanilla JavaScript. It filters out common words and combines core keywords without needing a backend server.

Why did it generate fewer hashtags than I requested?

If your input sentence is very short, the tool has fewer keywords to work with. Try writing a slightly longer, more descriptive sentence.
