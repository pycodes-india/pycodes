About This Passport Photo Maker

This free online tool lets you create a passport size photo (35mm x 45mm) directly in your browser. Upload any picture, crop it to the correct passport ratio, optionally add your name and photo date below the image, then download a print-ready PNG — no signup, no watermark, and no upload to any server, since every step runs locally on your own device.

How to Create a Passport Size Photo Online

  1. Upload a clear, front-facing photo using the "Upload Photo" button.
  2. Drag and resize the crop box so your face fits the passport frame correctly.
  3. Optionally toggle on the name and date section, then enter your full name and the photo date.
  4. Check the live preview on the right to confirm it looks correct.
  5. Click "Download Passport Image" to save the final 35x45mm photo as a PNG file.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard passport size photo dimension?

A standard passport size photo is 35mm x 45mm, which is the format this tool outputs (420x540 pixels, suitable for printing). This size is accepted for Indian passport, visa, and most similar government ID applications.

Is this passport photo maker free to use?

Yes, this tool is completely free with no signup or watermark. You can crop and download as many passport photos as you need.

Does this tool upload or store my photo on a server?

No. All cropping and image processing happens locally in your browser. Your photo is never uploaded to a server, which keeps it private.

Can I use this passport photo maker on my mobile phone?

Yes, the tool is fully responsive and works on Android, iOS, and desktop browsers without needing to install any app.

Can I add my name and photo date to the passport photo?

Yes, there is an optional toggle to include your name and date of photo (DOI) below the image. You can also turn it off to download just the plain cropped photo.

What file format is the downloaded passport photo?

The photo is downloaded as a high-quality PNG file at 420x540 pixels, matching a 35x45mm passport photo suitable for printing.
