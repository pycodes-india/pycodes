Frequently Asked Questions

What is Softbox?

Softbox is a free online software download center where users can find and download accounting software, web browsers, PDF tools, office suites, typing tutors, antivirus programs, and system utilities from one page.

Is Softbox free to use?

Yes. Browsing Softbox and accessing the software download links is completely free. Individual software licenses (such as trial or paid versions) are governed by their respective publishers.

What types of software can I download from Softbox?

Softbox offers accounting software (Tally, Vyapar, Busy, Marg ERP), web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, Opera), PDF tools (Adobe Acrobat Reader, Foxit Reader, iLovePDF), office suites (LibreOffice, WPS Office, OnlyOffice), typing tutors, antivirus tools, and file recovery utilities.

How do I download software from Softbox?

Go to the Softwares section, use the search box to find the app you need, and click its Download button. This takes you to the official publisher page or direct download link for that software.
