How to Convert VCF to Excel (XLSX) Online

Converting your mobile or email contacts into a spreadsheet format is simple. Follow these steps:

  1. Export your contacts: Save your contacts as a .vcf (vCard) file from your Android, iPhone, Google Contacts, or Outlook.
  2. Upload the file: Click "Choose File" above and select your exported .vcf file.
  3. Preview the data: Verify your extracted names, phone numbers, and emails in the preview table.
  4. Export to Excel: Click the green "Export to Microsoft Excel" button to download your new spreadsheet.

Is this tool secure and private?

Yes, 100% secure. Unlike other online converters, this PyCodes tool utilizes client-side JavaScript. This means your contact data is processed entirely within your own web browser. No data is ever uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any external servers, ensuring maximum privacy for your sensitive contact information.
